A balloon with the vague appearance of bank notes. I know that UNYU from Wallet of Unyu is supposed to be a cryptocurrency, but I wanted to design bills for it anyways. To see the full design, see the b2 or b8 sizes.

There are 6 possible colors, and each color comes in 5 sizes.

Inspired by Wallet of Unyu: https://github.com/nikolat/wallet_of_unyu
